<p>开奖活动</p>
<h1><b>Lottery</b></h1>

<p>玩法类似于大乐透</p>
<p><li>The gameplay is similar to the big lottery</li></p>

<p>开奖结果是每天中午12点开始连续n个MCI整百的主链单哈希值最后4比特，共n个4比特的数</p>
<p><li>The result of the lottery is the last 4-bits of the main chain single hash value of n consecutive MCIs starting at 12 noon every day (a total of n 4-bit numbers).</li></p>

<p>每一注是2TTT或者2000TFans，资金进入奖金池</p>
<p><li>Each note is 2 TTT or 2000 TFans, and the funds enter the bonus pool.</li></p>

<p>技术需求：钱包活动页面、机器人、开奖程序、批量打币程序</p>
<p><li>Technical requirements: wallet activity page, bot, lottery program, batch coinage program</li></p>

<p>运营操作流程：</p>
<p><li>Operational process:</li></p>

<ul>

<p>用户在钱包活动页面中下注：与机器人交互获得下注地址，选号并下注</p>
<p><li>i. The user should load the wallet activity page: interacts with the bot to get the bet address, selects the number and bets.</li></p>

<p>机器人汇总每期下注情况</p>
<p><li>ii. The bots summarize the betting situation in each period.</li></p>

<p>开奖前活动页面锁定，机器人汇总每期下注情况，包括地址和下注号码</p>
<p><li>iii. The pre-recruitment activity page is locked, and the bot aggregates the betting status of each period, including the address and bet number.</li></p>

<p>运营人员获取下注信息表</p>
<p><li>iv. Operator gets bet information table.</li></p>

<p>开奖时确定n个开奖MCI值，根据账本公布开奖结果和相应的单元哈希</p>
<p><li>v. Determine the n lottery MCI values at the time of the draw, announce the lottery results and corresponding unit hashes according to the ledger.</li></p>

<p>根据开奖结果筛选中奖者列表，包含地址和奖金金额</p>
<p><li>vi. Filter the list of winners based on the results of the draw, including the address and bonus amount</li></p>

<p>运营人员获取中奖信息表，并完成打款</p>
<p><li>vii. The operator obtains the winning information form and completes the payment</li></p>

</ul>
